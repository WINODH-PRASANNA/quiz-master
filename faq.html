How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple! Click the "Start" button in the navigation bar and follow the registration process. You'll need to provide a valid email address and create a password.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and enter your email address. You'll receive a link to reset your password. The link expires in 24 hours for security reasons.

How do I enroll in a course?

Browse our courses page, select the course you're interested in, and click "Enroll Now." Some courses are free while others require payment. You'll get immediate access after enrollment.

Can I take courses at my own pace?

Absolutely! All our courses are self-paced. You can complete them on your schedule and revisit the material anytime. Your progress is automatically saved.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and in some regions, mobile payment options. All transactions are securely processed.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid courses. If you're not satisfied, contact our support team within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.

What are the system requirements?

Quiz Master works on all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) on both desktop and mobile devices. We recommend using the latest browser version for best performance.

The quiz isn't loading. What should I do?

First, try refreshing the page. If that doesn't work, clear your browser cache or try a different browser. If the problem persists, contact our support team with details about your device and browser.

Still have questions?

Our support team is ready to help you with any other questions you might have.
